About FarmCast

FarmCast is designed to help farmers manage their farms more effectively. Our platform provides tools to track activities based on live weather forecast reports, manage tasks, and stay informed about the weather, all from a single, easy-to-use interface.

Main Features

Comprehensive Dashboard

Create your profile, add your farm information, add your farm activities and view them all in one dashboard

Activity Management

Plan,add and track your farm activities easily.

Weather Forecast

Stay updated with the latest weather information to plan your activities better.
